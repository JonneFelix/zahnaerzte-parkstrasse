## How much does a dental implant cost in Hamburg?

The cost of a single dental implant in Hamburg typically ranges from €1,800 to €3,500 — depending on the material, complexity and whether bone grafting is needed. At our practice in Othmarschen, we provide transparent cost information before any treatment begins.

## Why do prices vary so much?

Not all implants are the same. Costs depend on the **quality of the implant system**, the **surgical complexity** (simple implantation vs. bone augmentation), the **crown material** (ceramic, zirconia) and the **expertise of the specialist**.

At our practice, we use almost exclusively autologous tissue for bone augmentation. This is gentler and often more cost-effective than synthetic materials.

## The implant procedure step by step

**Step 1: Consultation and planning** — We examine your jaw, take X-rays and discuss your options.

**Step 2: Implantation** — The implant is placed into the jawbone under local anaesthesia. The procedure typically takes 30-60 minutes.

**Step 3: Healing phase** — The implant fuses with the bone. This takes 3-6 months.

**Step 4: Crown placement** — The final crown is placed on the implant. Done!

## Does health insurance cover the costs?

German statutory health insurance provides a fixed subsidy for dental prosthetics, regardless of whether you choose an implant or a bridge. The out-of-pocket cost for implants is higher, but the long-term benefits often outweigh the investment.
